Our elderly and their loved

NeuroSynch Health is a therapy center that guides and supports our elderly and their loved ones through the difficulties of neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer’s, Dementia, and Parkinson’s. If these diseases are left unattended, they can destroy an individual’s mental state so quickly that time plays a critical function in our current understanding of this debilitative disease. Currently, there are no medications that stop the degeneration of the brain. Therefore, it is crucial to use alternative methods that have been proven to slow degeneration to maintain memory and motor functions. NeuroSynch Health provides guidance and an integrative solution with multiple proven methods to slow degeneration; resulting in a major increase in mental and physical function. This helps our clients and families develop coping mechanisms to improve more than just the lives of our loved ones. Our team of professionals will then develop a personalized treatment plan for each person. This new and unique approach focuses on our clients’ objectives and personal interests in a manner utterly impossible to find in the current market.
